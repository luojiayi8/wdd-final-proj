What do I order?
You have no doubt pondered the differences in the fancy names of coffee drinks. Perhaps you are looking for something that will keep you awake.
Or something that will make you look professional at your meeting.
Or maybe something casual, like when you're meeting an old friend.
Or maybe you're here to figure out your go-to favorite drink, that one order that the barista knows to make the moment you walk in the door...
Or maybe you're intrigued by ho baristas make pretty designs in your coffee.
